Human freedom

... and does it relate to happiness?


The Human Freedom Index, created by the Cato Institute, is a 'global measurement of personal, civil, and economic freedom'. With over 100 indicators to choose from, and data from nearly every country in the world, we at brimless-yankee have handpicked a few indicators we find can be useful. With this visualization, one can compare indicators from the Human Freedom Index and the World Happiness Report. For more information, and access to the full set of indicators located within the Human Freedom Index, check out the Kaggle dataset or the Cato Institute website.
